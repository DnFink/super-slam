How To Play

Desktop Controls

  • ← → / A D Walk Left & Right
  • Space / W / ↑ / Z Jump (Hold for higher jump)
  • ↓ / S Warp Down Tubes / Pipes
  • Esc / P Pause Game

Mobile Controls

On mobile or tablet devices, use the responsive virtual joystick and D-pad on the screen!

Interactive Mechanics

Warp Pipes: Press ↓ on a green pipe to warp between connected parts of a level!
Moving Bombs: Watch out for walking green bomb enemies. Stomp them or let them walk into hazards!
Popping Coins: Smash yellow question blocks from underneath to release golden spinning coins.
Super Key Power-Up: Eat the Golden Key power-up to transform from tiny Owen into Super Owen!
