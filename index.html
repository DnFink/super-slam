How To Play

Desktop Controls

  • ← / A Move Left
  • → / D Move Right
  • ↑ / W Jump
  • ↓ / S Warp Down Vents
  • X Special Mode (When Ready)
  • C Shoot Fireballs (When Powered Up)
  • Esc / P Pause Game

Mobile Controls

On mobile or tablet devices, use the responsive virtual joystick and D-pad on the screen!

Interactive Mechanics

Warp Vents: Press ↓ on an industrial vent to warp between connected parts of a level!
Rogue Drones: Watch out for robotic drone enemies. Stomp them or let them drive into hazards!
Popping Coins: Smash yellow question blocks from underneath to release golden spinning coins.
Super Key Power-Up: Eat the Golden Key power-up to transform from tiny Slam into Super Slam!

Scoring

  • Coins +100 Points each (The best way to score high!)
  • Time Bonus +5 Points for every second left on the clock when finishing a level.
  • Stomping Enemies +200 Points
